KADALA RECIPE

  • Yield: Serves 4
  • Active Time: 50 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hours 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup black chickpea / kala chana, soaked overnight ½ tsp salt 4 cup wate
  • 1 tsp coconut oil ½ inch cinnamon 2 pods cardamom 3 cloves 1 tsp fennel seeds ½ onion, sliced
  • 2 clove garlic 1 inch ginger 1 tomato, chopped ½ cup coconut, grated few curry leaves ¼ tsp turmeric ½ tsp kashmiri red chilli powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder ½ tsp garam masala ½ cup water
  • 1 tsp coconut oil 1 tsp mustard 1 dried red chilli, broken few curry leaves 5 shallots, chopped 1 chilli, slit ½ tsp salt

Preparation

  1. 1.

    in a large kadai heat 1 tsp coconut oil and splutter 1 tsp mustard, 1 dried red chilli, few curry leaves.also add 5 shallots, 1 chilli and saute until the onions turn translucent.

  2. 2.

    in a large kadai heat 1 tsp coconut oil and splutter 1 tsp mustard, 1 dried red chilli, few curry leaves.also add 5 shallots, 1 chilli and saute until the onions turn translucent.

  3. 3.

    add in pressure cooked kala chana and ½ tsp salt.

  4. 4.

    mix well adjusting consistency as required. cover and cook for 15 minutes or until the flavours are absorbed well.

  5. 5.

    finally, kadala curry recipe is ready to serve with appam or puttu
