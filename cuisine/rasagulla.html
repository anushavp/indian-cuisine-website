Rasagulla Recipe

Serves Four

Ingredients

  • 1 litre milk, full cream
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 cup water
  • 1Â½ cup sugar
  • 8 cups water

Preparation

  1. firstly, in a thick bottomed pan add 1 litre milk. furthermore, stir occasionally and get to a boil.
  2. additionally, add lemon juice and stir well. add more lemon juice and stir till milk curdles completely.
  3. and immediately drain the curdled milk into the hand kerchief. pour a cup of water and clean the paneer as it has lemon juice in it.
  4. furthermore, bring it together and squeeze off excess water. hang for 30 minutes. or till all the water drains off completely.
  5. after 30 minutes, start to knead the paneer. knead paneer till it turns out smooth without any grains of milk.
  6. furthermore, make small balls of paneer and keep aside.
  7. drop the prepared paneer balls into boiling sugar syrup. cover and boil for 15 minutes. the paneer balls will have doubled in size.
  8. inally, serve rasgulla chilled or at room temperature garnished with few saffron strands.
