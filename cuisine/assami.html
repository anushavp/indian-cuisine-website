Assami fish curry Recipe

Serves Four

Ingredients

  • 500 gms fresh fish (Rohu)
  • 5 Potatoes (half boiled)
  • 4 Tomatoes 1 tbsp black mustard seeds 1 tbsp zeera
  • 4 tbsp fish masala
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 5 tbsp turmeric powder,mustard oil,Coriander leaves
  • 150 ml Salt (as per taste), 4 Green chillies

Preparation

  1. Marinate the fish with salt, turmeric powder, fish masala and 1 tbsp mustard oil. After 15 minutes fry the fish in mustard oil.
  2. Marinate the half boiled potato with salt, turmeric powder and fry it until it gets golden brown.
  3. Marinated the tomato with salt and turmeric and fry it in mustard oil.
  4. Put 50 ml mustard oil in a hot pan, once the oil gets hot put 1 tbsp zeera, 1 tbsp mustard seeds, 4 chopped green chillies and finely chopped onion.
  5. Fry all this until it gets little brown, put 1/2 tbsp turmeric powder, 1 tbsp fish powder and salt.
  6. Fry it for 5 minutes, pour 2 cups of warm water, covered the pan n put it in seam for 3-4 minutes.
  7. Put the fried fish into the pan. Keep it on for another 4-5 minutes.
  8. The recipe is ready, serve the fish curry with freshly chopped coriander and green chillies.
