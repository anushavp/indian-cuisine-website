TANDOORI CHICKEN

RECIPE

Marination cal.80

For marination add yogurt, red chilli powder, ginger garlic paste and salt to taste and marinate for 20 minutes

INGREDIENTS: cal.212

1 whole chicken, cut in 10-12 pieces - 200 gms Dahi/Curd - 1 tbsp Butter - 1 tsp turmeric powder - 2 tbsp red chilli powder - Salt to taste - 2 tbsp oil - 1 piece of charcoal - ginger garlic paste - Garnish - Coriander leaves - onions slices, - lemons halves
