ZUNKA RECIPE

  • Yield: Serves 4
  • Active Time: 50 minutes
  • Total Time: 1 hours 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp oil 1 tsp mustard 1 tsp cumin / jeera
  • pinch hing / asafoetida 4 clove garlic, finely chopped 1 chilli, finely chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped ¼ tsp turmeric 1 tsp kashmiri red chilli powder 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup besan / gram flour water as required 2 tbsp coriander, finely chopped

Preparation

  1. 1.

    firstly, in a large kadai heat 2 tbsp oil and splutter 1 tsp mustard, 1 tsp cumin and pinch hing. also add 4 clove garlic, 1 chilli and saute well.

  2. 2.

    now add 1 onion and saute until the change colour slightly. further add ¼ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp chilli powder and 1 tsp salt. saute slightly, without burning the spices.

  3. 3.

    now add 1 cup besan and saute on low flame. saute for 5 minutes, or until the besan turns golden and aromatic. further, sprinkle ¼ cup water and mix well.

  4. 4.

    keep sprinkling water in batches until the mixture turns moist. i have sprinkled almost 1 cup water in batches. cover and simmer for 10 minutes, or until the besan is cooked completely.

  5. 5.

    give a good mix making sure there are no lumps. finally, add in coriander leaves and enjoy zunka with bhakri.
