SAMBAR RECIPE

  • Yield: Serves 4
  • Active Time: 25 minutes
  • Total Time: 2 hours 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • ½ cup toor dal / arhar dal / split pigeon pea 2 cups water ¼ tsp turmeric powder / haldi 1 cup radish / mooli / mulangi, chopped 5-6 pieces drumstick ½ cup carrot, chopped
  • 1 cup tamarind juice 1 green chilli. slit lengthwise ¾ to mato, finely chopped few curry leaves ½ tsp turmeric powder / haldi
  • ¾ tsp jaggery ½ onion, cubed salt to taste 1 tbsp sambar powder
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil / cooking oil ¾ tsp mustard seeds / rai
  • pinch of hing / asafoetida 2 dried red chilli few curry leaves

Preparation

  1. 1.

    firstly in a pressure cooker take half cup washed toor dal. furthermore add 2 cups of water. or water as required to cook dal

  2. 2.

    then place a small bowl into a pressure cooker.add veggies (radish, carrot, drumstick) or vegetables of your choice into the bowl.do not add any water. then cover the cooker and pressure cook for 5 whistles on medium flame.

  3. 3.

    firstly, in a large kadai squeeze 1 cup of tamarind juice. furthemore, add chilli, tomatoes, curry leaves and turmeric powder. also add jaggery . then get the tamarind water to boil.

  4. 4.

    further boil for a minute and switch off the stove. prepare the tempering by heating oil in a small kadai.

  5. 5.

    further add mustard seeds, hing, red chilli and curry leaves.

  6. 6.

    finally, give a good mix to vegetable sambar and serve with hot steamed rice.
